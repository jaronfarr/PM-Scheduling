The Compliance Gap Dilemma

Historically, the dominant approach to PM has been strict calendar or usage-based intervals dictated by static manuals. This creates a massive "Compliance Gap" between an O&M strategy and field reality.

Over-Maintenance

Wasting highly constrained labor hours on healthy assets simply because a static 6-month calendar trigger was activated.

Under-Maintenance

Missing high-risk, catastrophic failures because vital pre-failure signs ("dark data") are buried in unstructured technician notes.
